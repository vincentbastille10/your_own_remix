FAQ

What happens with the rights?

You keep the rights to your original track. The exact terms for the remix (royalties, label split, etc.) are agreed together depending on your project (self-release, label, contest).

Can you also make a radio edit?

Yes. If you need both a club version and a shorter streaming / radio edit, this can be discussed depending on your needs and timeline.

Which language do you work in?

Communication can happen in English or French. The service is open to artists, labels and producers worldwide.

Can you start from just a vocal?

Yes. Many remixes start from a strong vocal or acapella. The more clean and isolated the vocal, the better.
